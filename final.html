Sea Sheperds Official Site

The Sea Shephers is a non-profit marine conservation group founded in 1977 by Paul Watson. The group was created to battle illegal whaling and hunting of indangered marine life.They are a non-profit group that go around the world to governments that ignore these situations trying to persuade them to bring laws on the topic. If the government shows no signs of action they take responsibilty into their own hands.

The group is known to use unorthodox methods to stop whaling vessels such as throwing stink bombs on board, shooting ships with water canons and trapping hunter ships inbetween their own boats to even putting their own ships in the hunters path.
